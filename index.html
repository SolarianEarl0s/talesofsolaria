Welcome to Solaria

Tales of Solaria is an epic fantasy universe brought to life through cutting-edge AI technology. By leveraging tools like Sora for visuals, Suno for scores, and OpenArt for character design, we've created a world of magic, conflict, and destiny.

🎙️

The Origin

"This all started from voice recordings years ago, on a holiday to Ireland — an imagination of a bunny hopping through the woods. From that single spark of wonder, the entire Tales of Solaria series came to be."

From a simple voice recording to a full transmedia universe, created with cutting-edge AI tools and boundless imagination.

Legendary Sagas

Book 1: Evin

A farmer who fights for his people, but carries a hidden depth that readers won't see coming.

A young nature mage thrust into leadership while grappling with grief and an unstable inner power.

Book 3: Sepa

A tribal chief's son struggling with confidence as Rifts threaten to swallow his world whole.

Book 4: Unity

The last standing hero, armed with a weapon of destiny as catastrophic energy threatens the sky.

Book 5: The Prince

A young royal in the floating kingdom of Koh-i-Noor who discovers the rot within his kingdom's soul.
