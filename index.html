Act I · Phase One

The Beginning

The video that jumpstarted the entire project. Images were AI-generated using ChatGPT, then character and scene elements were manually cut out using the Picsart mobile app. At the time, I was adapting Act I of Tales of Solaria: Evin's Tale, with the source document and a clear creative vision already in hand. Short clips were created and posted to TikTok (@Solarianearl), with the full scenes then compiled and uploaded to YouTube. The only editing tool used at this stage was InShot, for simple keyframing, cutouts, and transitions.

Watch on YouTube ↗

Act I · Phase One · Continuation

The Cliffhanger

A continuation of the Act I, Chapter 1 scene, ending on a deliberate cliffhanger. The intention was to produce more videos in this style, though limited tools and experience at the time made it difficult to take further.

Watch on TikTok ↗

Act I · Phase Two

Fully Realised

This was the version I was most eager to bring to life as a fully realised scene. Unlike Act 3.5, Jack's Final Message, this was a project I genuinely invested in from the start. With the full book series on Wattpad, I knew the plot from beginning to end and had a clear vision of how I wanted it to play out. The main obstacle at this stage was Sora 2's unpredictable content moderation. Sourcing all the individual clips, particularly the battle sequences between characters, was a genuine challenge, but working through those limitations made the final result all the more rewarding.
