The Saga Begins

🐰

"This all started from voice recordings years ago, on a holiday to Ireland — an imagination of a bunny hopping through the woods. From that single spark of wonder, the entire Tales of Solaria series came to be."

From a simple recording to a six-book transmedia universe, Tales of Solaria is brought to life through cinematic direction and original scores.

Tales of Solaria is a developed IP where cinematic director Adrian Carter combines deep worldbuilding with technical production. Leveraging Sora for visuals and Suno for audio, we create a world of spiritual pressure, environmental distortion, and raw emotion.
