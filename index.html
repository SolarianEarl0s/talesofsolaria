Act I · Phase One

The Beginning

The video that jumpstarted the entire project. Images were AI-generated using ChatGPT, then character and scene elements were manually cut out using the Picsart mobile app. At the time, I was adapting Act I of Tales of Solaria: Evin's Tale, with the source document and a clear creative vision already in hand. Short clips were created and posted to TikTok (@Solarianearl), with the full scenes then compiled and uploaded to YouTube. The only editing tool used at this stage was InShot, for simple keyframing, cutouts, and transitions.

Watch on YouTube ↗

Act I · Phase One · Continuation

The Cliffhanger

A continuation of the Act I, Chapter 1 scene, ending on a deliberate cliffhanger. The intention was to produce more videos in this style, though limited tools and experience at the time made it difficult to take further.

Watch on TikTok ↗

Act I · Phase Two

Fully Realised

This was the version I was most eager to bring to life as a fully realised scene. Unlike Act 3.5, Jack's Final Message, this was a project I genuinely invested in from the start. With the full book series on Wattpad, I knew the plot from beginning to end and had a clear vision of how I wanted it to play out. The main obstacle at this stage was Sora 2's unpredictable content moderation. Sourcing all the individual clips, particularly the battle sequences between characters, was a genuine challenge, but working through those limitations made the final result all the more rewarding.

The Cast

Meet the souls who shape the fate of Solaria.

E

Evin

"He's not the hero you think he is."

The One Who Was Never Just a Hero

A farmer who fights for his people against those with power over them. On the surface, that sounds familiar. It isn't. Evin is easy to misread, and that's exactly the point.

J

Jack

"He chose love over everything."

The Anchor Who Chose Love

A wandering musician whose power flows through rhythm and belief. Jack doesn't fight for the world, he fights for her. When faced with an impossible choice, he gives everything.

R

Rose

"She was never meant to be saved."

The Hero Who Was Never Meant to Be

A fierce protector, admired as a hero, but built on something broken. With her anchor gone, what remains is not just a fallen hero, but something far more dangerous.

U

Unity

"She made the choice no one else could."

The One Who Has to Let Go

A loyal friend who stood beside them both, never the strongest, but always the one who stayed. When everything falls apart, Unity is left with the choice no one else could make.

Juan Souls

"He already knows how it ends."

The Dancing Death

A twisted entity that sees what others can't: the future. He doesn't guess. He doesn't hope. He knows.

Alara

"She was there... before and after."

The Seer. The Mentor. The Sister.

A quiet, mysterious presence seen only in fragments in Act I. She appears when the world begins, and again when it nears its end.

Ar

Arvel

"He lost everything. He kept going."

The One Who Carries the Weight

Arvel doesn't wear his burden loudly. He carries it quietly, and that's what makes him dangerous. His story isn't about power. It's about what someone does when they've already lost everything.

Sepa

"The shield only holds if you believe in it."

The Shieldbearer. Book III.

Sepa carries the weight of his tribe and the doubt that weakens his shield. To contain the Rift, he must first believe in himself.
